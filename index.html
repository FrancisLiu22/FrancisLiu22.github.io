About

We conduct research on nanoscale superconducting devices within the Department of Physics in the University of Illinois At Urbana-Champaign. The topics of interest include superconducting memory, superconducting qubits, hybrid topological superconducting systems and quantum phase transitions in nanowires and thin films. Our typical research project include nanofabrication in the clean room, low noise and microwave experiments, and theoretical modeling of superconducting systems.
